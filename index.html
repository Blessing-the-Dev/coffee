French roast

French roast coffee refers to a particular type of dark roast coffee. As its name suggests, French roast has a high roast level, resulting in coffee beans that have a roasted flavor, and contrary to other light roast popular types of roasted coffee, Frenchroast coffee does not have an acidic flavor.
